    • What is Bethel Family Choir ?

    a christian choir founded in 2005 in Groupe Scolaire Officiel de Butare (Indatwa n' Inkesha School) Huye,Rwanda. It is choir made up of youth students at GSOB.Its activities take place in Groupe Protestant which is Protestant church in GSOB.Its activities are mainly involved with praise and worship.

    Bethel logo

    Ubumwe,Ukuri n' Urukundo : byerekana inkingi Bethel igenderaho

    Iseri ry'umuzabibu : nyuma yuko umuntu ageze muri Bethel aba agomba kubwirisha ubutumwa bwiza ni imbuto yerera bagenzi be nkuko yacu ibivuga BICIYE MURI TWE IMANA YIGARAGARIZE ABANDI

    Igitereko cy'itabaza : Dushingiye k'ubutumwa bwiza uko bwanditse na Matayo 5:14 :"Muri umucyo w' isi" niyo mpamvu Bethel twashimiye kotubishyira muri logo yacu ngo bihere bitwibutsa ko tugomba kumurikira isi.

    Irembo riri mu gicu: Nkuko twabisobanuye haruguru Bethel bibiliya itubwira ko ari irembo ry'ijuru twifuje ko rero natwe twaba irembo riyobora mu ijuru nkuko umurimo wacu uri.

    Bibiliya : Bibiliya yerekana ubutumwa tuvuga mu ndirimbo ko tubuvana mu ijambo ry'imana.

    Umucyo uva k'umusaraba : byerekana ko kuba bica muritwebwe kugirango Imana yigaragarize abandi,yuko natwe dufite isoko nka "Bethel" biturukaho iyo ntayindi ni umucyo twahawe ubwo Yesu yatwatangiraga.

    Amanota y'umuziki: nyuma yuko twakora ibindi byose ntitwa kwibagirwa icyotwa hamagariwe ariwo murimo woguhimbaza Imana no kuyiramya binyuze mu ndirimbo.

    Uruzitiro (uruziga): Twifuje ko bimwe tugendera ho muri Bethel tubizitirisha uruziga rufatanye rwerekana ubumwe n'urukundo muri Bethel nkumuryango "Bethel niferme".
