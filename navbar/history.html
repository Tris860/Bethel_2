    • History of Bethel

    Bethel yatangiye 2002 ari abanyeshuri bashimaga Imana buri wa gatanu mu buryo bw' indirimbo nyuma baza guhinduka itsinda ry' abaririmbyi ryari rishinzwe kuramya no guhimbaza

    2003 nibwo yagize umuyobozi wayo wambere,witwaga MUSAFIRI Jean de Dieu.We yahagaze 2003 ,Bethel yaje gusabwa koyaba Chorale ntiyabyemera ,kuko yo yifuzaga ko yaguma ari GROUPE DE LOUANGE ET ADORATION

    2004 president MUSAFIRI ntiyagarutse kwiga bibangombwa ko Jean Paul aba president yiga mu mwaka wa kabiri

    2005 Bethel yaje gura n'imbogamizi zuko yatangiye kwangirwa kuririmba ndetse nuko ntamuyobozi yagiraga muri komite y' itorero tariki 17/5/2005 nibwo yaje kwemererwa kuba Chorale n'ubuyobozi bw' Ikigo hamwe n' itorero, ari nabwo yiswe BETHEL CHOIR, 2016 nibwo yongerewe ho kwitwa FAMILY

    2008 ,abaririmbyi bari barangije banzeko icyo Imana yabahamagariye kirangirira muri G.S.O.B batangiza minisrty yitwa BETHEL MINISTRY Muri uwo mwaka nibwo bashyize ho n'intego ya BETHEL: "Binyuze muritwe Imana yigaragarize abandi"

    2012 haje gushyirwa ho logo ya Chorale BETHEL. Bethel yajo gukorerwa isabukuru yayo yambere y'imyaka irindwi (7 years B-Anniversary). Muri uwo mwaka ninabwo hatangiye umwanya wa Sport muri Bethel bise B-Sport

    2013 haje gutangizwa competition yogukina umupira w' amaguru yiswe B-CHAMPION

    2015 haje kugurwa uniforme nshya ya chorale

    2016 hateguwe icyumweru cyo gushima Imana kumyaka BETHEL imaze.
